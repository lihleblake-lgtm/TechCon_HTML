History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of passionate developers and has since grown into one of the most anticipated technology Conferences worldwide. Each year, it brings together experts, students, and industry leaders to share insights and inspire innovation.

Mission

Our mission is to foster collaboration, celebrate diversity in tech, and inspire the next generation of innovators. TechCon is dedicated to providing accessible learning opportunities, hands-on experience, and meaningful connections.

Notable Past Speakers

Over the years, TechCon has welcomed some of the brightest minds in the industry:

  • Ada Lovelace = Renowned for her pioneering work in computing and inspiring women in tech.

  • Alan Turing - Celebrated for his groundbreaking contributions to artificial intelligence.

  • Grace Hopper - A visionary computer scientist and advocate for programming languages.
